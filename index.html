Construction Types

Understand each category and where it's commonly used in the real world.

Frame

Wood-framed homes, often seen in residential areas. Cost-effective but highly flammable and less structurally durable in fire situations.

Joisted Masonry

Brick/block exterior with wood framing inside. Common in older retail or apartments; decent fire resistance with moderate cost.

Non-Combustible

Steel or concrete materials throughout. Ideal for factories and large stores; strong resistance to fire spread.

Masonry Non-Combustible

Brick/block exterior with concrete or metal roofs/floors. Widely used in commercial buildings like warehouses or offices.
